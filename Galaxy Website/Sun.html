● Potential for Life

• The Sun could not harbor life as we know it because of its extreme temperatures and radiation.

• Yet life on Earth is only possible because of the Sun’s light and energy.


● Size and Distance

• Our Sun is a medium-sized star with a radius of about 435,000 miles (700,000 kilometers).

• Many stars are much larger–but the Sun is far more massive than our home planet:

• it would take more than 330,000 Earths to match the mass of the Sun, and it would take 1.3 million Earths to fill the Sun's volume.
